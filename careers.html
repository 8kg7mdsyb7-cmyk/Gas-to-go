Why Work With Us?

We believe happy employees make happy customers. Here's what we offer every person on our team.

💰

Competitive Pay

We start above minimum wage and give regular performance-based raises. Work hard and get paid.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Whether you're a student, parent, or just need to work around your life — we work with your schedule, life gets supper busy.

🍔

Free Shift Meals

Enjoy a free hot food item and drink every shift you work. Clean out the store!

📈

Growth Opportunities

🎓

Tuition Support

Supportive Culture

Life at Gas to Go

A Team That Feels Like Family

When you join Gas to Go, you're not just getting a job, you're joining a family. We celebrate birthdays, share meals, and check in on one another.

Our founder Mia leads with openness. Her door (and DMs) are always open. Team feedback shapes how we do things around here — we listen and we act on it.

Whether you're working the morning rush or closing out the night shift, you'll always have a team behind you ready to help out and make the shift a good one.
