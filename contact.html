Common Questions

FAQs

Are you really open 24/7?

Yes! The store and fuel pumps never close. You can fuel up at 3 AM or grab a midnight snack whenever the craving hits.

How do I join the Rewards program?

Just ask any cashier or sign up on our website. It's completely free and you start earning points on your very first purchase.

Do you offer diesel fuel?

Absolutely! We carry Ultra Low Sulfur Diesel at competitive prices. We have dedicated diesel lanes for trucks and larger vehicles.

Is there parking beyond the fuel pumps?

Yes! We have a dedicated parking lot with 20+ spots for customers who want to come in and shop without fueling up.

Do you accept all credit cards?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. We want paying to be easy!

Can I suggest a product to carry?

We love suggestions! Use the contact form above or tell any team member in store. Many of our products got here because customers asked!
