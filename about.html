Where It All Began

Started with a Dream,
Fueled by High Gas Prices

Gas to Go was born from a simple idea: a gas station that actually cares. Gas prices were way too high (and there was a certain final that needed to be completed) so the only solution was to make a gas station that cares.

Where the care goes beyond the pump. Where the coffee is always fresh, the food selection is amazing, and every customer can feel at home.

We believe a great gas station is about more than fuel. It's about the experience — the friendly wave at the register, the perfect hot dog at 2 AM, the clean restroom you actually want to use. That's the Gas to Go promise.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Early 2024

The Idea is Born

The idea of Gas to Go was born after fueling up for gas one day and realising it was way too expensive.

Mid 2024

Location Secured

After months of searching, and bribing a couple of banks, the perfect spot is found. Renovations begin right away.

Late 2024

Grand Opening!

That was fast. Gas to Go opens its doors. All of the country shows up (9 people but who's counting?)

Early 2025

Loyalty Program Launches

We introduce the Gas to Go Rewards program — customers earn points on every purchase and redeem them for fuel and snacks.

2026 & Beyond

Growing Strong

We're expanding our hot food menu, adding EV charging stations because electric cars exist, and looking for passionate people to join our growing team.
